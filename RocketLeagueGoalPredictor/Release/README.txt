How to install this plugin:
1. Find where you have BakkesMod installed by opening the BakkesMod application and clicking `File -> Open BakkesMod folder`, usually something like `C:\Users\[you]\AppData\Roaming\bakkesmod\bakkesmod`.
2. Copy the contents of the included `bakkesmod` folder to your installed `bakkesmod` folder.
3. Launch Rocket League, open the BakkesMod menu (`F2`), go to `Plugins` tab, open `Plugin Manager`, and enable `Goal Predictor`.
